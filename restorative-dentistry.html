Restorative Dentistry

Restore Your Teeth and Gums and Get Back to Smiling

We all wish our natural teeth could last a lifetime, but a lifetime can throw a lot at your smile. Whether you are dealing with discoloration, cavities, chips, cracks, stains, or missing teeth, we can restore your smile using inlays and onlays, crowns and bridges, dentures, and other techniques. With your teeth restored, you will get back the function you have lost along with a more youthful appearance.

Inlays & Onlays

When tooth decay advances to the point where a regular filling isn’t enough, you can consider inlays and onlays. These treatments are less invasive than crowns and can strengthen your teeth for decades to come.

Crowns & Bridges

If you are missing one or more teeth, we can close the gap in your smile using crowns and bridges. Get back to chewing, speaking, laughing, and loving your full smile. We use the most sophisticated materials in our crowns and bridges, ensuring results that last.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Are you missing a lot of teeth? Even with extensive damage, it is possible to get back to a luminous, full smile. Our dental experts can combine treatments in a personalized plan to fully rehabilitate your mouth. Soon, you’ll be looking in the mirror at a whole new smile.

Dentures

Patients looking for a cost-effective, non-invasive option for replacing many lost teeth can consider dentures. Our dentures are fully customized to your teeth and gums, and fit so well that you’ll be astonished how comfortable you feel wearing them.

Dental Implants

If you are missing a tooth, we can insert a dental implant and crown to close the gap in your smile and restore the function. The state-of-the-art materials and technologies we use ensure long-lasting results.

Tooth Extractions

No one likes to hear that it is time to remove a tooth, but sometimes, it is the best option for preventing the spread of an infection or changes to your bite. Our gentle dental experts work hard to make tooth extraction as fast, easy, relaxing and painless as possible.
