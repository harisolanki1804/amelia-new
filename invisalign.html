Clear Aligners or Invisalign

The Discreet Way to Straighten Your Teeth

What if there were a way you could align your teeth without having to wear braces?

Traditional braces work well, but there is no denying they come with some inconveniences. Many people do not like the appearance of metal in their mouths. Plus, flossing and eating while wearing regular braces can pose some challenges. They also can cause ulcers and mouth soreness and the wire components may hurt.

Clear aligners or Invisalign solves all of these problems! This modern alternative to traditional braces involves wearing transparent aligners periodically to effect change in the position of teeth and ultimately give you a beautiful smile.

You can hardly see them, and you can take them in and out whenever you want.

What are Clear Aligners? Invisalign? Are there other brands?

Clear aligners are custom made aligners which are transparent. Invisalign is one of the major brands which manufactures ... these aligners. There are many options and different brands available in the market today. Invisalign being the pioneer in this field is the most popular one. In Australia, the other brands which are popular are Smile Styler, Angelalign, Clear Correct, Sure Smile, etc. All these use a patented software to treatment plan and digitally simulate the teeth correction. A special resin material is used manufacture the aligners. The results achieved with any of them is excellent and comparable to Invisalign provided the case has been treatment planned well.

These clear aligners are used as a set for Upper and lower teeth and are changed every two weeks for optimal results. The number of sets of aligners needed are determined by the difficulty of the case and the amount of correction required. Each sequential aligner applies progressive forces on your teeth as a whole to facilitate the movement of teeth into a desired configuration gradually.

Unlike braces, Clear aligners are not bonded to your teeth, nor is there any metal.

Instead, you have clear aligners that fit over your teeth which are completely customized to you.

At a glance, most people will not even notice you are wearing them.

Clear Aligners can be used to treat a variety of malocclusions which include:

• Crowded teeth – teeth which are crooked and overlapping ...
• Diastema correction – to close gasp between teeth
• Rotated teeth – teeth which have turned around their own axis.
• Overbite or Deep bite correction – when the upper teeth overlap more than 2 mm.
• Proclined teeth – Teeth which are placed forward more than normal.

Nowadays clear aligners can also be routinely used for severe cases of malocclusion which require repositioning of the jaws or which require extractions.

Are You a Candidate for Clear Aligners?

Clear Aligners may be right for you if:...

• You want to straighten your teeth or correct your bite, but do not like the feel or look of traditional look.
• You do not smoke (doing so can stain the aligners).
• You are willing to wear your aligners 22+ hours every day.

Not sure if Clear Aligners or traditional braces are best for you? During your consult, we can discuss the pros and cons of each with you.

Benefits of Clear Aligners

• You can hardly see Clear aligners. You don’t need to worry about a dramatic shift in your appearance while you are using them. ... Say goodbye to the idea of a mouth full of metal!
• You can eat anything you want. You get to remove the aligners whenever you want, so you can enjoy foods that are impossible to eat with braces—yes, even corn on the cob.
• Brushing and flossing is so easy. Trying to floss around traditional braces is a challenge. But with Invisalign, you just take out the aligners and brush and floss the way you always do.
• Clear Aligners doesn’t get in your way. Whether you want to play a musical instrument or enjoy a kiss with that special someone, you can just take the aligners out for a little bit.
• Skip the adjustment appointments you would need for regular braces.
• There is no worrying about broken wires or brackets. When a wire breaks or a bracket dislodges on traditional braces, you have to come and get it fixed as soon as possible to ensure continuity of treatment.
• Keep your confidence high. Many people struggle with their self-esteem while wearing old-fashioned braces. Clear Aligners lets you smile with pride.

How Clear Aligner Work

1. We start with a consultation where we take a scan of your teeth or impressions to make moulds of your teeth. We also take ... intraoral and extra oral photos and the OPG and the lateral cephalogram xrays. We then create the orthodontic problem list and come up with the best personalized plan for aligning your teeth.

2. Next, we send your scans and all the xrays and photographs to the aligner company for a computer simulation of your treatment plan. Our recommendations and treatment goals are enlisted to them.

3. An company expert then creates a simulation of your treatment by recommending the number of aligners and attachments. This personalised treatment plan then is then resent to us for approval. This step usually takes about a week.

4. Review of treatment plan. At this appointment, you will get an opportunity to see the complete set up and computer simulation of your proposed treatment. The number of aligners required for the full treatment will also be included. If you are satisfied by the computer simulation, you consent and approve the treatment plan along with the dentist.

5. After approval, the custom aligners are then fabricated. We then receive the full set of aligners in a couple of weeks.

6. Insertion and issuance of aligners. The 1st set of aligners is inserted and issued to you and the treatment begins. We will make sure that they fit snugly. All instructions are given to you

7. You wear your aligners each day, removing them only while brushing and eating food. They have to be worn for 22+ hours each day. Usually the aligners are worn for 2 weeks subsequently followed by the next set.

8. A follow-up appointment with the dentist is done every 4 weeks to ensure the fit and progress of treatment. It is compared to the computerised treatment plan and reviewed.

9. Occasionally your treatment may have to be revised and new aligners may need to be fabricated.

How to Maintain your Clear Aligners

To keep your Invisalign aligners in good condition: ...

• Take your aligners out before you eat. Always rinse them with cold or lukewarm water after removal.
• Do not clean them with hot water because heat can damage and warp the plastic. You can use a mild detergent.
• Rinse your aligners before you store them in their case. Let them dry.
• Do not leave them lying around outside their case especially if you have pets at home. Pets will often chew up the aligners and damage them.
• Continue brushing and flossing your teeth as you always do.

After Invisalign

After your treatment period is over, you can maintain your new smile with a clear retainer also called as the Essix retainer. ... Retention is the most important part of your clear aligner treatment. The long-term success is dependent on wearing your retainers religiously for atleast 22+ hours for more than 12 months initially. Additional wear may be advised on an individual basis.
