Our Differences

The Amelia Street Dental Difference

Are you new to Amelia Street Dental Clinic? You may be wondering what sets us apart from other dental clinics.

Our key differences come down to our core values - our caring staff, the personal care and family atmosphere. We have state-of-the-art facilities and most importantly our dentists’ have close to 30 years’ experience each.

Through every aspect of what we do, we work hard to make your visit a relaxing one where you feel listened to, well cared for and completely at ease. We make sure that each patient experience right from the first point of contact to the actual treatment provided by the dentist’s is exemplary and compassionate.

Our Values

Our core values can be summarized by our tagline, Enlighten, Empower, Enrich:

Enlighten

We educate each one of you about your oral health and dental needs. We lay a lot of emphasis on teaching the young and the old the importance of taking the responsibility of their own oral and dental health. We ensure that we teach you the corelation of oral and general health.

Empower

Our dental experts offer you personalized recommendations for you to be proactive in protecting and maintaining your dental health. We impart the necessary knowledge and means to manage your oral care effectively and judiciously

Enrich

By empowering you with the techniques and practices you need to maintain your oral health or improve it, we aim to enrich your quality of life now and in the years to come. By delivering excellent comprehensive oral and dental care, we will enrich your life to confidently enjoy it to the fullest.

Our Compassionate Staff

Our dentists, assistants, and office staff are all passionately committed to providing the highest level of care to patients of all ages. Their sole focus is on taking care of you at every level.

We are a family-owned and operated business run by the caring husband-wife dentist team (Dr Abhijit (Jeet) and Dr Vijaya Wagh). We strive to treat our staff and our patients as members of our family, and we will always treat you with respect, care, kindness, and love. This is our promise to each one of you.

That means that when you walk through our door, we spend time asking you questions and getting to know you, understanding you and your circumstances. We take time to answer all your questions too.

Most importantly, we listen closely to everything you have to say without judgement. That way, we can offer you affordable treatments that are completely personalized to fit your needs and goals for oral health.

Our Front Office and Waiting Area

From our comfortable and bright waiting room which offer TV, reading materials, games, and refreshments to our comfy and spacious dental operatories with overhead TV, soothing music, and wi-fi, you will feel right at home at Amelia Street Dental. The Front office staff will make you feel at ease as soon as you walk in. We follow all the guidelines laid down by the Australian Dental Association and this ensures the safety of all our patients as well as the staff. Our staff and reception are very well trained for this purpose.

Our Location

Did we mention we also make it easy for you to find us? We are easily accessible by public transport. The public transit bus stop is exactly opposite the Northlands Shopping Complex on Amelia Street in Balcatta which is bang outside our clinic location. There is plenty of free parking available for those using their own vehicle. We cater to the needs of the disabled by offering easy wheelchair access into the waiting room as well as the surgeries.

Our Flexibility

• We book your appointments according to your needs and will work with you to schedule around any hindrances. If you have a dental emergency, we can get you in immediately to prevent complications and provide you with rapid relief of your symptoms.

• Get the care you need now, pay later. We accept all major health funds and government schemes. If you cannot afford your gap payment upfront, ask us about the National Dental Plan powered by HUMM, which provides zero-interest financing up to $12,000. We do offer in-house payment plans under special circumstances.

• Multilingual service and support. We have staff fluent in English, Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Konkani, German, Bahasa, Arabic, Assyrian, and Neo-Aramaic.

Accreditation

Amelia Street Dental Clinic is proud to state that it is an Australian Dental Association and QIP (Quality, Innovation, Performance) accredited dental practice as per the NSQHS standards for dental care.

QIP accredited means that patients, consumers, and clients know that there is consistent high level of dental care is given to all our patients. It ensures peace of mind, safety, and quality.

So be assured and confident about the care and treatment you will receive at Amelia Street Dental Clinic.
