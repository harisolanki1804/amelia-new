Orthodontics

The Straight Smile You Always Wanted Can Be Yours

When your teeth are crooked, your bite can be uneven, making it hard to chew your food or even producing pain. Moreover, you may not be in love with the look you see in the mirror. But you can be, once you have visited Amelia Street Dental for orthodontic services. To make teeth straightening easy and convenient for every patient, we offer both Invisalign (Clear Aligners) and traditional braces.

Invisalign or Clear Aligners

Have crooked teeth, but don’t like the way that traditional braces look? Invisalign may be right for you. These transparent aligners are a fast, convenient option for straightening your teeth. Most people probably will not even notice you are wearing them. They also make it super easy to brush, floss, and eat all your favourite foods.

Braces

Along with Invisalign, we also offer traditional fixed orthodontics (braces) here at XYZ Dental. Fixed orthodontics may be right for you if you want to straighten your teeth if the treatment required is more complicated or deemed to be lengthy.
