About Us

Welcome to Amelia Street Dental. We are looking forward to learning more about you during your first appointment. Looking to learn more about us? Let’s get to know each other. On this page, we share our story, mission, and values.

Our Story
Amelia Street Dental was started in 1980 with a simple goal: providing easy, comfortable, and affordable dental treatments for the whole family under one roof. At the end of 2018, the ownership changed to the dentist duo of Dr’s Abhijit aka Dr Jeet and Dr Vijaya, a husband wife team who envisaged the same philosophy of providing complete family dental care in a compassionate and caring way.

With more than 50+ years of combined experience between them, our dentists have the specialized expertise to provide a wide range of general, restorative, and cosmetic treatments for patients of all ages.

Since going into business, our clinic has become a favourite among patients in Balcatta, Nollamara, Westminster, Stirling, Perth, and the surrounding area. Check out some of our 5 Star Reviews.

Our
Mission

Every person has the right to strong, beautiful teeth and healthy gums to last a lifetime. Our mission at Amelia Street Dental is to provide you with the care you need to enhance or maintain your oral health at any age.
Not only do we work hard to give you a healthy, beautiful smile, but we also strive to give you something to smile about. Our friendly, welcoming staff are here to make your experience with us an enjoyable one from start to finish. You’ll be amazed how relaxing dental care can be. We want you to head home feeling like you’ve had a trip to the spa!

Our
Values

Here at Amelia Street Dental, our slogan is “Enlighten. Empower. Enrich.” These three words capture the values that infuse all of the work we do to keep our patients smiling.

Enlighten

We know you may have many questions about your dental health. Our caring experts are here to patiently listen to each one of you and answer each of your concerns or queries and to provide you with personalized recommendations for taking care of your teeth, gums, and oral health. We will enlighten you about the latest in contemporary dentistry and make you confident about your dental and oral health.

Empower

The daily self-care you do at home is one of the most important factors in your lifelong oral health. Our well-trained dental staff are here to teach you the most effective ways to brush, floss, and maintain dental treatments. They will empower you with all the current knowledge that you need to keep your oral health in a mint condition.

Enrich

Through our routine appointments like Check-ups and cleans or your specific treatment appointment, we seek to enrich your oral health and your life, making it possible for you to laugh, talk, eat, and smile with confidence. Live life to its fullest with healthy teeth and gums and a vibrant smile. Be confident and assured that this enrichment will make your teeth last a lifetime.
