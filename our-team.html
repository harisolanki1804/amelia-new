Meet Our Founders

Dr. Abhijit Wagh

BDS, MDS, ADC (Melbourne), Dental Surgeon

Dr. Abhijit Wagh, known as Jeet, is the founder and senior dentist at Amelia Street Dental Clinic.... With over 25 years of experience, he specializes in endodontics, clear aligners, orthodontics, and advanced cosmetic dentistry.

Dr. Jeet is dedicated to providing positive dental experiences for patients of all ages. As a member of the Australian Dental Association and founder member of the Australian Clear Aligner Society, he is committed to mentoring overseas dental graduates. At Amelia Street Dental Clinic, Dr. Jeet and his team strive to enlighten patients about their oral health, empowering them with knowledge to enrich their overall well-being. They look forward to welcoming you to their practice.

Dr. Vijaya Wagh

BDS, MDS, ADC (Melbourne), Dental Surgeon

Dr. Vijaya Wagh, co-founder and senior dentist at Amelia Street Dental Clinic... brings 27 years of expertise in general dental practice. Graduating from Government Dental College, University of Mumbai in 1994, she further honed her skills in Prosthetic Dentistry. After owning a successful private practice and teaching in Pune, she completed her Australian Dental Council examination in 2010. Dr. Wagh’s gentle demeanour and expertise make her adept at managing children and nervous patients. Committed to providing the highest standard of dental care, she specializes in cosmetic dentistry, kids’ dentistry, and a wide range of procedures including wisdom teeth removal, root canal treatment, and dental implants.

Dr. Wagh’s passion lies in smile designing and cosmetic dentistry, where she helps patients enhance their smiles and confidence. Her dedication to professional development ensures she stays updated with the latest techniques and technology, including completing an upgradation course in Dental Implantology. Outside of work, Dr. Wagh enjoys outdoor activities with her family, gardening, cooking, and singing with Shamira Entertainment, a Perth-based Bollywood music orchestra. She is a member of various prestigious dental associations like:

1. Australian Dental Association
2. Australian Prosthetic Society
3. Indian Prosthetic Society
4. Indian Dental Association
