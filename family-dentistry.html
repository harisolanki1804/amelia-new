Family (General) Dentistry

Dental Services for the Whole Family

Do you look forward to dental appointments? Once you have had your first appointment with Amelia Street Dental, we think you will. We make dental care easy, convenient, and relaxing by providing all dental services and treatments for the whole family under one roof

Cleanings, Exams and Periodontal Disease Maintenance

At Amelia Street Dental, we make routine cleanings and exams fast and easy. With regular appointments, you can prevent tartar build-up, prevent and treat cavities, and avoid invasive dental procedures. Our friendly and reassuring dentists / hygienists will make the experience a relaxing one.

Root Canal

Suffering from a painful infection or abscess in your tooth? With a root canal treatment, we may be able to save your tooth and take away the pain.

Wisdom Teeth Removal

If your wisdom teeth are impacted, it is important to act swiftly to prevent crowding, infection, pain, and complications. We can surgically extract your wisdom teeth in the dental chair to stop pain and prevent further complications.

Fillings

If you have a cavity, getting it filled as soon as possible is the key to keeping tooth decay from advancing. We offer composite fillings and fluoride-based glass ionomer fillings that strengthen your tooth and blend right in. Best of all, our dental experts are able to get the job done quickly and painlessly.

Kids Dentistry

As general dentists with experience and compassionate care, we are well equipped to handle any dental treatment need of your child. We routinely do extractions, fillings, space maintainers, habit breaking appliances and mouth guards.

Oral Cancer Screening

Screening regularly for oral cancer is essential since the condition is often undetectable to patients during the first stages. By giving you a comprehensive check, we offer you the best chance at early detection and successful, life-saving treatment.

TMJ & Bruxism

The pain of TMJ and teeth grinding (bruxism) can make it hard to chew, interfere with quality sleep, and plague your days with pain. Together, we can create a customized plan to treat your teeth grinding and TMJ to reduce pain, restore restful sleep, and get you back to eating all your favourite foods.

Custom Mouth Guards

Do you play footy, or any contact sports? Tired of over-the-counter mouth guards that just don’t fit the way they should? By taking impressions of your teeth and gums, we can create a custom fit mouth guard to protect your teeth, gums and jaws. Our perfect fit mouth guards will offer you the best protection during sport. It will be fabricated in 2 days.

Occlusal splints and Night guards

Do you grind your teeth, have TMD? Are your teeth or facial musculature sore? Well then its time to get a custom made occlusal splint or a night guard. We make your special splint that is dual laminar and is soft towards the surfaces of the teeth and hard on the exterior to prevent any permanent damage to your teeth due to grinding.

Dentistry for Seniors

At Amelia Street Dental, we take care of every dental need of the senior patient. It will include everything from routine exams, scale and clean, fillings, extractions to dentures and implants and everything in between
