Our Treatments

Our Treatments for Teeth, Gums and Oral Health

At Amelia Street Clinic, we offer a full range of dental services under one roof, including preventative care, exams and diagnostics, and treatments for acute or chronic conditions. Below, you can learn about all of our services for patients in Perth and the surrounding area.

Family (General) Dentistry

If you are looking for treatments that help you to maintain or improve your oral health, our family dentists can ensure that your appointment is smooth, fast, easy,and painless. We offer the following general dentistry services:

  • Routine Dental Hygiene appointments
  • Periodontal Disease Maintenance
  • Root Canal Treatment
  • Wisdom Teeth Removal
  • Oral Cancer Screening
  • TMJ & Bruxism
  • Mouth Guards
  • Tooth Extractions

Restorative Dentistry

Do you have one or more missing teeth, or are your teeth cracked or damaged? Restorative dentistry can bring your smile back to its full glory. Amelia Street Clinic offers these restorative treatments:

  • Inlays & Outlays
  • Crowns
  • Bridges
  • Full Mouth Rehabilitation
  • Dentures
  • Dental Implants

Orthodontics

Correct your bite alignment and straighten out your teeth with orthodontic treatment. Before you know it, you will be looking in the mirror at a beautiful straight smile.

  • Invisalign and Clear Aligners
  • Braces (Traditional and cosmetic)
  • Myofunctional appliances

Cosmetic Dentistry

Crooked teeth, cracked teeth, or yellowed or stained teeth? Whether you want a total smile makeover or you just want to correct one unsightly blemish, our cosmetic dental treatments can restore your smile and your confidence.

  • Teeth Whitening
  • White Fillings
  • Ceramic Veneers
  • Smile Designing
  • Smile Makeovers

Paediatric Dentistry (Kids Dentistry)

Protect your children from cavities and help them learn healthy habits for oral hygiene by scheduling an appointment for paediatric dentistry. Our dentists are gentle and compassionate, so your child feels safe and comfortable through the entire visit.

  • Paediatric fillings
  • Paediatric extractions
  • Habit breaking appliances for Thumb sucking, mouth breathing, tongue thrusting etc.
  • Interceptive Orthodontics for correction of early malocclusion
  • Space maintainers
  • Fissure sealants

Emergency Dental Care

Severe dental pain, impacted wisdom teeth, and other dental emergencies require immediate treatment. Book one of our special daily emergency slots to get the prompt and instant care you need for fast relief of your pain symptoms.

  • Emergency tooth extraction
  • Emergency root canal treatment and Pulp extirpation for immediate pain relief.
  • Emergency Abscess drainage
  • Management of dental trauma (Fixing of fractured or chipped tooth, avulsed tooth, dental fracture.)

Prosthetic Dentistry

Do you have one or more missing teeth and expensive dental implants or ceramic bridges are out of reach. Prosthetic dentistry can bring your smile back to its full glory. Amelia Street Clinic offers these prosthodontic treatments:

  • Complete Dentures or Full Dentures
  • Removable Cast Partial Dentures
  • Removable Acrylic Partial Dentures
  • Removable Valplast Dentures
