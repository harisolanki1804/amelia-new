Enhance and Beautify Your Smile With Ceramic Veneers

Are some of your teeth discoloured, crooked or oddly shaped? Are there chips or gaps? Do you try to keep your mouth closed when you smile, and constantly worry that your teeth are making a bad impression?

At Amelia Street Dental Clinic, we’ll give you something to smile about! In fact, we can transform your smile completely using ceramic veneers. It’s fast and easy, and the results are dramatic and confidence boosting.

What are Ceramic Veneers?

Veneers are quickest way to a beautiful and perfect smile. This treatment modality is perfect for those who have teeth ... which are stained, chipped, have gaps or are crooked or overlapping. This being an irreversible procedure, it is important to know all the information, choices and potential risks associated. Discuss with our dentist if this is the right procedure for you and the benefits associated with it.

Veneers can be described as thin shells or facings made up of ceramic or composite resin material which are bonded to your teeth. Our dental experts create a custom set for you in a lab and are contoured and shaped in such a way that a natural homogenous smile is created. They cover the fronts of the teeth and look just like real enamel.

Veneers are a more conservative option than crowns

Veneers can be made of ceramic or a composite material. Ceramic veneers are also called “porcelain veneers.”

We recommend ceramic over composite veneers because unlike composite veneers, they are not prone to discoloration.

Are You a Candidate for Ceramic Veneers?

We can transform your smile with ceramic veneers if: ...

• You want to make cosmetic adjustments to your teeth.
• You are looking to cover up problems like stains, chips, worn enamel, gaps, and misalignment.
• You want to make other adjustments in appearance (for example, you do not like the sizes or shapes of some of your teeth).
• You do not require more extensive work.

In some situations, we may recommend a smile makeover or full mouth rehabilitation instead of ceramic veneers.

During your consult, our dental experts can help you figure out the right procedure for you.

How We Create Your Custom Ceramic Veneers

You will be amazed by how quick and easy it is to get outfitted with a set of custom ceramic veneers. ...

1. The process starts with an in-depth consultation.
We want to know exactly how you feel about your smile. What do you want to change? What do you want to stay the same? Based on what you tell us, we can come up with a custom plan for your ceramic veneers.
2. We take a set of intra oral and extra oral photos, impressions or scans of your teeth and then use digital smile designing to create a mock-up of your proposed smile. If you are satisfied by the computer simulation, we then fabricate a set of temporary veneers for you. During this step, we also shave down your enamel to prep the tooth surfaces for the veneers. Less than a millimeter of shaving is required.
If there are things you want to change about the temporary veneers, let us know so we can adjust the look of your final veneers.
3. Once you are happy with the temporary veneers, we scan or take impressions of your prepared teeth and send it to the dental laboratory for final fabrication.
4. We advise our patients to see our local dental technician based in Perth for shade selection and let him know your expectations.
5. Come in receive your final veneers. We will make sure that the fit of the veneers is perfect, and that you are happy with the shape and colour. If any last-minute adjustments are needed to fine-tune the veneers to your liking, we will do so now.
6. The last step is to bond the ceramic veneers permanently to your teeth. The transformation is instant. Now your perfect smile is yours to enjoy!

How to Maintain Your Ceramic Veneers

If you take excellent care of your ceramic veneers, they can last for up to two decades. Maintaining your veneers is easy: ...

• Practice good oral hygiene. Brush, floss, and use mouthwash.
• Make regular trips to the dentist for cleanings.
