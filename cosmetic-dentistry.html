Cosmetic Dentistry

It’s Time to Fall in Love With Your Smile

Are you the person who nervously keeps your mouth closed when you smile for photos? You are a beautiful person who deserves to feel confident that your smile is every bit as magnetic as you are. Amelia Street Dental offers a variety of quick cosmetic solutions or cosmetic treatments that can help you transform your smile, including teeth whitening, ceramic veneers, white fillings, and full smile makeovers.

Teeth Whitening

If time and stains have taken their toll on your teeth, it is time to restore them to their natural whiteness.... Our fast and easy teeth whitening procedure will put the glow and shine back in your smile. We offer In-Chair teeth whitening for an immediate result or Home-Teeth whitening which may take a few weeks for the desired result.

White Fillings

The old days of unsightly metal fillings are behind us. At Amelia Street Dental, we can give you composite white ... fillings to match your teeth. These fillings blend so well that it won’t be long before you forget you ever had them. These are commonly used for routine restorative and cosmetic dentistry.

Ceramic Veneers

Put the days of crooked and discoloured teeth behind you with ceramic veneers. This rapid cosmetic procedure ... can cover up flaws for the brilliant white smile you have always desired. Give your appearance and your confidence a massive boost. With good bonding systems, ceramic veneers are the treatment of choice for improving orbeautifying your smile.

Smile Makeovers (Smile Design)

Tired of looking at crooked or fractured teeth, gaps, and stains? Maybe it is time for a complete smile makeover.... Together, we can work to create your ideal smile, using procedures such as white fillings, Invisalign, inlays and onlays, and ceramic veneers. Soon, you will love the smile that greets you in the mirror. With support from our renowned local dental laboratory, we can do a digital mock up and simulation of your improved smile for you to see prior to the actual procedure
