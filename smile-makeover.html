Smile Makeovers

Get Ready to Love Your Smile with a Smile Makeover

“Say cheese!” When people ask you to smile for photographs, do you find yourself wishing you could hide? You might be worried about how your crooked, stained, chipped or missing teeth will look in a photo. Or you might be bothered by how your old fillings look. Or perhaps your teeth are not lined up fully and are making you conscious of your smile.

You deserve to love your smile, and to show it off with pride in every photograph. The experts at Amelia Street Dental Clinic are here to give you the smile you have always wanted with a total smile makeover.

What is a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover is a process where we transform your smile from one ... that may be marred by stains, fractures, misaligned teeth, and old fillings, into one featuring sparkling, straight teeth that shine

Are You a Candidate for a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover may be right for you if: ...

• Your teeth feature yellow or brown stains or discoloration.
• Some of your teeth are broken or fractured.
• You are missing one or several teeth.
• You have unattractive old fillings.
• Your teeth do not line up correctly.
• You are not in love with the smile you see in the mirror.
Have you lost many teeth, or are you suffering from severe gum disease? In cases like these, we recommend full mouth rehabilitation instead. A smile makeover is appropriate when existing damage to your teeth, gums and surrounding bone is mild to moderate or when most of the oral and dental status is stable with healthy gums and minimal caries or restorations.

When you come in for your consultation, we can help you figure out if you are a candidate for a smile makeover or if full mouth rehabilitation would be more suitable.

Benefits of a Smile Makeover

• Give your teeth and gums a more even, symmetrical, and beautiful appearance. ...
• Whiten and brighten your teeth.
• Fill in gaps.
• Replace or cover up unsightly or deteriorating old restorations.
• Restore teeth that are chipped or fractured, improving their appearance and strength.
• By reducing the effects of age on your teeth, you can make your entire face look more youthful.
• Pleasing Smiles help you make a great first impression and is vital in boosting your confidence and self-esteem.

How We Give Your Smile the Ultimate Makeover

When you visit us for your smile makeover consult, we will come up with ... a personalized plan just for you. With our Intraoral scanner we can generate a 3-D image of all your teeth and gums in a few minutes. The digital software and digital mock-ups is then used to do your Digital Smile Designing. We can show you the improvements which can be made. Some of these procedures we might perform include:

• White fillings or composite restorations.
• Ceramic veneers
• Clear aligners or Invisalign
• Fixed orthodontics or braces
• Metal free Ceramic Crowns and bridges.
• Metal free Ceramic Inlays and Onlays

How simple or comprehensive the process is, depends entirely on your mouth and your goals for your appearance. We believe in “Minimally Invasive Dentistry” which essentially means preserving as much of your original teeth and modifying the teeth as less as possible and making treatment plans around them to achieve the desired outcome.

Maintaining Your New Smile

Once you have a glowing new smile, how do you keep it looking bright and perfect? ...

• Brush and floss each day
• Rinse with mouthwash regularly
• Avoid bad habits like smoking that discolour your teeth
• Regular 6 monthly periodic Check-ups and hygiene appointments
• Avoid very crunchy, hard, or sticky food.

Just by following regular good habits for oral hygiene, you can enjoy your new smile for many years to come.
