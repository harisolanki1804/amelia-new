Apply for “No Interest Ever” Financing for Immediate Dental Treatment.

Are you uninsured, or does your health fund or insurance not cover dental treatment? That does not have to stop you from getting the immediate oral care you need. Ask us about the National Dental Plan, and you could start your treatment today.

What is the National Dental Plan?

The National Dental Plan is your path to immediate dental treatment, even if you do not have the finances to cover the full cost upfront....

Powered by HUMM, this specific dental financing plan is interest-free forever. Over 5 million Aussies have used this plan to get the dental treatments they needed to maintain or improve their oral health. Now, it is your turn.

Features and Benefits

• Finance up to $12,000 AUD.
• Borrow a minimum of $500 AUD....
• Apply in 5 minutes.
• Find out instantly if you are approved.
• Use your financing for any type of dental treatments.
• Repay on a fortnightly schedule with direct debit.
• Payment terms up to 24 months are available.
• Very easy application process.
• Integrated with our practice management software D4W.

Do I Qualify?

• Australian Citizens and Australian Permanent Residents are welcome to apply....
• Must be at least 18 years of age
• You must have an Australian Driver’s License.
• You need to be working 25 or more hours each week.
• Have a current and valid debit card in your name.
• Your credit history may influence approval.
